About Me

My name is Henry Ohler, and Dialed By H started from a genuine obsession with watches.

I came into this space as a collector first. I spent years learning references, studying market shifts, and understanding why certain pieces matter beyond the hype. What began as a personal passion naturally turned into friends asking me to help source watches they could not find or did not feel comfortable buying on their own.

That trust is what built Dialed By H.

Today, I work with a vetted network of global partners and personally oversee the process from authentication and condition verification to pricing and secure delivery.

Dialed By H is not a storefront. It is a concierge service built on trust, discretion, and access. Whether you are chasing a hard to find reference or looking to move a piece from your collection, I approach each transaction as if it were my own.
