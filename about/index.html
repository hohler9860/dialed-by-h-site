About me

Dialed By H Henry Ohler

I’m Henry. Dialed By H started because I was obsessed with watches before I ever sold one.

I came in as a collector first. Years of learning references, watching the market, and figuring out which pieces are actually worth chasing past the hype. Friends started asking me to find watches they couldn’t get to or didn’t trust buying alone. That’s how this started.

That trust is what built Dialed By H.

I work with a vetted network of dealers, collectors, and private sellers across the US, Europe, and Asia. I’m personally on every piece from sourcing through authentication, pricing, and delivery.

Dialed By H isn’t a storefront. It’s a one-person concierge built around trust, discretion, and access. Whether you’re chasing a hard-to-find reference or looking to move a piece from your collection, I treat every deal like it’s mine.
