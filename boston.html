Unlike traditional Boston authorized dealers with multi-year waitlists, our concierge service connects you directly to a global sourcing network for faster access to the exact reference you want.

How is a watch concierge different from a Boston watch dealer?

A traditional watch dealer in Boston carries limited in-store inventory and may require waitlists for popular references. A watch concierge like Dialed By H works on your behalf, sourcing globally from vetted partners, independently authenticating every piece, and delivering at competitive pricing without the retail markup or allocation games.

Yes. Dialed By H helps Boston collectors sell luxury watches including Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, and more. Is watch consignment available in Boston?
