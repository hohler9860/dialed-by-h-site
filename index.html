Tell me what you're looking for

FAQ

I work through a vetted network of dealers, collectors, and trusted contacts across Boston, New York, Miami, and globally. When you tell me what you're looking for, I tap that network directly instead of waiting for a piece to surface publicly. Most sourcing requests get a real lead within 12 hours.

Every piece is independently authenticated by a trusted watch specialist before it reaches you. I verify provenance, papers, and movement. If anything doesn't check out, the deal doesn't happen.

Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, F.P. Journe, A. Lange & Söhne, Vacheron Constantin, Richard Mille, Cartier, and the independent watchmakers most dealers don't carry. If you have a brand in mind that isn't on this list, ask. Chances are I can still find it.

First response on a sourcing request: within 12 hours. Time to actually find the watch varies by reference. Common references can land in days. Hyped or discontinued pieces may take a few weeks. I keep you updated the whole way.

Yes. I offer competitive market trade-in pricing toward your next piece, and I can also sell a watch outright on consignment. Either way, you get a transparent quote and independent authentication.

I'm based in Boston with active presence in New York and Miami. Pieces ship fully insured anywhere in the United States. International delivery is case by case. Reach out and we'll figure it out.
