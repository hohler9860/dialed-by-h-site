My Process

~12 Hours

Consultation

You tell me what you're looking for: brand, reference, condition, budget. I listen, advise, and set realistic expectations.

1 – 2 Days

Sourcing

I activate a global network of trusted dealers, collectors, and private sellers. Leads are vetted for provenance and history.

~24 Hours

Authentication

Pieces are independently authenticated by trusted watch specialists. Dial, movement, case, and bracelet are verified.

~24 Hours
