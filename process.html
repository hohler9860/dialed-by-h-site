My Process

01

Consultation

We start with a conversation. You tell me what you're looking for — brand, reference, condition, budget. I listen, advise, and set realistic expectations based on current market availability.

02

Sourcing

I activate my global network of trusted dealers, collectors, and private sellers. Every lead is vetted. I only pursue pieces with clean provenance and verifiable history.

03

Authentication

Before any transaction moves forward, every piece is independently authenticated by trusted watch specialists. Dial, movement, case, bracelet — everything is verified.

04

Delivery

Once authenticated, the piece is fully insured and shipped via secure courier. You receive detailed documentation, photography, and tracking every step of the way.
